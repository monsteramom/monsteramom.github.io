Plant Care Guide

Expert care instructions for all your Monstera Mom plants

Monstera Deliciosa

A large tropical foliage plant with iconic split leaves (Swiss Cheese Plant). Native to Central America and commonly grown as a statement indoor plant.

See Details
▼

Watering

Water deeply only after the top 2 to 3 inches of soil have dried 💧 This plant prefers a wet–dry cycle rather than constant moisture 🚿 In winter or low light, reduce watering significantly to prevent root rot 🌿

Light

Grows best in bright indirect light ☀️ Can tolerate medium light but fenestration and leaf size will reduce 🌤️ Prolonged direct sun can scorch leaves 🌱

Soil & Fertilizer

Requires a chunky, fast-draining soil mix 🪴 An aroid mix with orchid bark, perlite, and coco coir works best 🌱 Roots need airflow to stay healthy 🧪

Propagation

Propagate using stem cuttings with at least one node ✂️ Roots easily in water, moss, or soil 💧 Warm temperatures speed rooting 🌱

Arrow Head

A fast-growing vine with arrow-shaped leaves (Syngonium podophyllum). Adaptable and popular for indoor trailing displays.

See Details
▼

Watering

Keep soil lightly and evenly moist 💧 Allow the top inch to dry between watering 🚿 Reduce watering slightly in winter while avoiding full dry-out 🌿

Light

Prefers bright indirect light ☀️ Tolerates low light but leaf shape and color become less distinct 🌤️ Avoid direct sun 🌱

Soil & Fertilizer

Likes loose, well-draining soil that retains some moisture 🪴 Standard mix with added perlite works well 🌱 Soil should never compact 🧪

Propagation

Very easy to propagate from node cuttings ✂️ Roots well in both water and soil 💧 Regular pruning encourages bushy growth 🌱

Croton

A bold ornamental plant with striking multicolored foliage (Codiaeum variegatum). Sensitive to stress and environmental changes.

See Details
▼

Watering

Water thoroughly once the top soil dries 💧 Never allow the root ball to dry completely 🚿 In winter, reduce frequency but keep moisture consistent 🌿

Light

Needs very bright light to maintain color ☀️ Can handle some direct sun indoors 🌤️ Low light causes dull leaves and drop 🌱

Soil & Fertilizer

Requires rich, well-draining soil 🪴 High-quality potting mix with added perlite is ideal 🌱 Excellent drainage is critical 🧪

Propagation

Propagated via semi-woody stem cuttings ✂️ Best done in warm, humid conditions 💧 Rooting hormone improves success 🌱

Pink Princess

A variegated philodendron with pink splashes (Philodendron erubescens ‘Pink Princess’). Grown for contrast rather than speed.

See Details
▼

Watering

Water only after the top 2 inches of soil dry 💧 Overwatering leads quickly to root rot 🚿 In winter, watering should be minimal and controlled 🌿

Light

Requires bright indirect light ☀️ Insufficient light reduces pink variegation 🌤️ Direct sun can burn lighter leaf areas 🌱

Soil & Fertilizer

Prefers a chunky, fast-draining aroid mix 🪴 Bark, perlite, and coco coir are ideal 🌱 Roots need oxygen more than moisture 🧪

Propagation

Propagate using stem cuttings with a node ✂️ Variegation is genetic and not guaranteed 💧 Moss or water rooting works best 🌱

Swiss Cheese Monstera

A smaller-leaf Monstera with oval holes (Monstera adansonii). Commonly grown as a trailing or climbing houseplant.

See Details
▼

Watering

Water when the top soil layer feels dry 💧 Slightly more forgiving than Monstera deliciosa 🚿 Reduce watering during winter dormancy 🌿

Light

Thrives in bright indirect light ☀️ Tolerates medium light well 🌤️ Direct sun can scorch thin leaves 🌱

Soil & Fertilizer

Needs light, airy soil 🪴 A mix with bark and perlite prevents compaction 🌱 Keeps roots oxygenated 🧪

Propagation

Propagates easily from stem cuttings ✂️ Roots quickly in water 💧 Transfers well to soil 🌱

White Princess

A variegated philodendron with white splashes. Grown for contrast rather than speed, with coloration dependent on strong light and stable care.

See Details
▼

Watering

Water when the top 30–40% of the soil has dried 🌱 Overwatering leads quickly to root rot 💧 Reduce watering in winter and allow longer dry periods 🍂

Light

Bright indirect light ☀️ Insufficient light reduces pink variegation 🎨 Avoid harsh direct sun, which can scorch lighter leaf areas 🚫

Soil & Fertilizer

Chunky, fast-draining aroid mix 🌿 Bark, perlite, and coco coir are ideal 🪵 Good airflow around roots is essential 🌬️

Propagation

Stem cuttings with at least one node ✂️ Variegation is genetic and not guaranteed 💧 Moss or water propagation works best 🌱

Philodendron Burle-marx

A compact, crawling philodendron valued for its durability, tidy growth habit, and ease of care.

See Details
▼

Watering

Water when top 30–40% of soil has dried 🌱 More forgiving than Alocasia 💧 Avoid standing water 🚫

Light

Bright indirect light ☀️ Tolerates medium light but grows leggy 🌿

Soil & Fertilizer

Chunky, fast-draining aroid mix 🌱 Slightly more organic matter tolerated

Propagation

Stem cuttings with at least one node ✂️ Roots easily in water or moss 🌱

Alocasia Macrorrhiza Splash

A dramatic giant Alocasia with white splash variegation. Grown for bold foliage and contrast, but demanding and sensitive to care consistency.

See Details
▼

Watering

Keep soil evenly moist but never soggy 💧 Allow top 20–30% to dry 🌱 Extremely sensitive to overwatering and cold roots ❄️ Reduce watering slightly in winter

Light

Bright indirect light ☀️ Can tolerate gentle morning sun 🌤 Low light reduces splash ❌

Soil & Fertilizer

Very chunky, fast-draining aroid mix 🌱 Bark, perlite, coco chips ideal 🪵 Drainage is critical

Propagation

Division or corm propagation 🌱 Variegation is genetic but not guaranteed 🎨

Philodendron Birkin

A compact self-heading philodendron with pinstripe variegation. Easygoing but light dependent for pattern.

See Details
▼

Watering

Allow top 30–40% to dry 💧 Avoid soggy soil ❌ Reduce watering in winter

Light

Bright indirect light ☀️ Can tolerate medium 🌤 Low light reduces striping ❌

Soil & Fertilizer

Chunky aroid mix 🌱 Bark, perlite, coco chips ideal 🪴

Propagation

Stem cuttings with at least one node ✂️ Roots easily in water or moss 🌱

Calathea White Fusion

A highly variegated Calathea prized for white and green foliage. Stunning but demanding, requires consistent care and humidity.

See Details
▼

Watering

Keep soil evenly moist 💧 Never allow to fully dry 🌱 Extremely sensitive to tap water and cold 🧊 Reduce watering slightly in winter

Light

Bright indirect light ☀️ No direct sun ❌ Low light dulls variegation

Soil & Fertilizer

Light, airy mix 🌱 Coco coir, perlite, fine bark ideal 🪴 Must retain moisture without staying soggy

Propagation

Division only 🌱 Variegation not guaranteed

Philodendron Jose Buono

A large, dramatic variegated philodendron with splashy cream patterns. Vigorous grower with proper warmth and light.

See Details
▼

Watering

Water when top 25–30% dries 💧 Sensitive to cold roots 🧊 Reduce watering in winter

Light

Bright indirect light ☀️ Gentle morning sun tolerated 🌤

Soil & Fertilizer

Very chunky aroid mix 🌱 Large bark, perlite, charcoal 🪴 Drainage critical

Propagation

Stem cuttings with at least one node ✂️ Roots easily in water or moss 🌱

Monstera Thai

A slow-growing Monstera with creamy speckled variegation. Premium plant requiring patience and proper soil.

See Details
▼

Watering

Water when top 25–30% dries 💧 Extremely sensitive to root rot ❌ Reduce watering in winter

Light

Bright indirect light ☀️ Essential for variegation 🌱

Soil & Fertilizer

Very chunky aroid mix 🌱 Bark-heavy, fast draining 🪴

Propagation

Node cuttings 🌱 Variegation stable
