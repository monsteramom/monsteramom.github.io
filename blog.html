From Two Tiny Leaves to a Home Full of Green

This is the story of how our love for plants grew into something bigger: a belief that no house truly becomes a home without a little green life inside.

It all started with one little Monstera. It had only two tiny leaves, and we were new to the plant world.

A couple of months later, the room was full of houseplants. My husband caught the plant fever too, and now our baby loves reaching for every leaf close enough to touch.

We decided to share that joy with other homes, because a little green life can make a space feel warmer, softer, and more alive.
