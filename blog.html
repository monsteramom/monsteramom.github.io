From Two Tiny Leaves to a Home Full of Green 🌱

This is the story of how our love for plants grew into something bigger, a belief that no house truly becomes a home without a little green life inside.

It all started with this monstera 🌱

It was just a little plant with only two tiny leaves. I was new to the plant world.

And then, I do not know how, but after a couple of months I found myself surrounded by a room full of various kinds of houseplants 🪴

My husband also caught the plant fever from me 😄

And we have a 6-month-old who loves to hold every plant leaf he can reach 👶🍃

We decided to take our love to the next level.

To share our love with other people, with other homes.

Because we believe no house becomes a home without a plant 💚
