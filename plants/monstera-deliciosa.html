Care Guide

Growing Notes

Water

Water deeply after the top 2 to 3 inches of soil dry. It prefers a wet-dry rhythm over constant moisture.

Light

Bright indirect light supports larger leaves and fenestration. Avoid harsh afternoon sun.

Soil

Use bark, perlite, and coco coir so the roots can breathe and drain quickly.

Propagation

Take a stem cutting with at least one node and root it in water, moss, or soil.
