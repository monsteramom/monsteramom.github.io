Care Guide

Growing Notes

Water

Water after the top 2 inches dry. Avoid wet soil, especially in cool rooms.

Light

Bright indirect light helps hold pink variegation without scorching pale areas.

Soil

A chunky mix with bark, perlite, and coco coir protects roots from staying wet.

Propagation

Use a node cutting. Variegation is genetic and each new leaf can vary.
