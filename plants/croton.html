Care Guide

Growing Notes

Water

Water when the top soil dries, but avoid letting the root ball fully dry out.

Light

Very bright light keeps leaves colorful. Low light often causes leaf drop.

Soil

Use rich potting mix with perlite and a pot that drains freely.

Propagation

Root semi-woody stem cuttings in warmth and humidity.
