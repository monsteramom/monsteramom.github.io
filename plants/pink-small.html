Care Guide

Growing Notes

Water

Small pots dry faster, so check soil regularly and water only after partial dry-down.

Light

Bright indirect light gives the best chance of attractive variegation.

Soil

Use a small-pot aroid mix that drains fast and does not compact.

Propagation

Allow the plant to size up before taking cuttings.
